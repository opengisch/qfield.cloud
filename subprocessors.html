QFieldCloud sub-processors

For further legal and compliance information see legal & compliance

The SaaS app.qfield.cloud is a service offered by QFieldCloud GmbH, a Swiss company based in Laax.

To support the delivery of our services, we may engage third-party service providers ("sub-processors") who process data on our behalf. This page outlines the sub-processors we currently use, along with a description of their services and locations. We are committed to ensuring that all sub-processors are carefully selected and meet our security and privacy standards.

Exoscale

Exoscale is running the datacenters on which processing of the main data takes place and provides storage.
