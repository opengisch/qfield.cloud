QFieldCloud BETA transition informations

Dear QFieldCloud BETA user, We are very excited to announce that we are ready to say goodbye to the beta status of QFieldCloud! On XX.XX.2023 we will release QFieldCloud for general availability.

We started work on QFieldCloud in 2020, and after innumerable hours of development, testing, discussing and challenging decisions - one year later, we released the first sources under the MIT license. In April 2022 we released QFieldCloud Beta and thanks to your and 40K other early adopters we were able to identify and fix plenty of issues and implement new features. We now have reached a point where we feel confident to leave the Beta world behind and focus on running the best possible service for you.

Now it's time to say Thank You!

Without your effort in testing QFieldCloud and your honest feedback, we would not be where we are today. We are incredibly thankful for all the feedback we received from you. To thank you for this, we decided to offer everyone switching to a paid plan before the XX.XX.2023 a whopping 40% discount for a full year!

How does the transition work?

To be eligible for the SuperEarlyBee offer, you simply need to go to the billing page in your profile, upgrade to a paid plan and add your payment method. Everything will be then automatically activated and your first invoice will be issued on XX.XX.2023. Between the moment you put your payment details down and the first invoice you will immediately enjoy all the benefits of your future plan. Will be in a temporary "Beta pro" or "Beta org" plan

Still questions?

Join us in our QFieldCloud AMA (ask-me-anything) on Friday, 3 February 11:00 - 12:00 where you can ask QFieldCloud and QField developers any questions you might have.

Bye bye BETA! Hello seamless fieldwork!
