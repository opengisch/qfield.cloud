QFieldCloud Terms of service

QFieldCloud is currently in the process of being BETA tested. Do not use it with production data.

All data is stored on servers hosted in Switzerland.

During the Beta phase, each user gets a free Team account with all functionalities enabled.

One month before the end of the Beta Phase, we will inform you to decide what plan you would like to subscribe to.

It is important to note that the Beta program comes with no availability or data durability guarantees; it is intended as a way to give early feedback during the final stretch of the product's development, not to run production workloads. As a means of maintaining the necessary development velocity, OPENGIS.ch reserves the right for unannounced maintenance windows and tearing down environments during the early adoption phase. Any change in policy will be notified to members of the BETA program.

For more information, please refer to our newsletters.
